# Hack The System: A Resounding Success!

The API Association of Proactive Innovators recently hosted a highly successful cybersecurity seminar titled **Hack The System: Offensive Security & Ethical Hacking** on **October 7, 2024**. 

Held at the **Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao (OSS Learning Commons)**, the event drew students interested in cybersecurity. The day's agenda was filled with engaging discussions and hands-on activities designed to deepen participants' understanding of both offensive and defensive cybersecurity strategies.

## Meet Our Expert Speaker

We were honored to have **[Jasper Van Marcellones](https://dracozona.com)** as the keynote speaker for this exciting event. Jasper is a seasoned cybersecurity consultant specializing in offensive security, with vast experience in vulnerability identification, penetration testing, and implementing robust security measures. His passion for advancing the field of cybersecurity as both a consultant and a researcher is evident in his work.

As the founder of **Dragon Zecurity** (formerly Dragon Network Security), Jasper has worked with organizations around the world to strengthen their defenses against modern cyber threats. His commitment to staying ahead of emerging threats is why he is such a sought-after expert in the field.

## **Highlights from the Seminar**

The event covered a variety of critical cybersecurity topics, from the fundamentals to live hacking demonstrations. Here’s a snapshot of what attendees experienced:

### **Key knowledge areas covered:**
- **Basics of Offensive and Defensive Cybersecurity**  
  Jasper broke down the key differences between offensive and defensive approaches, providing invaluable insights for both beginners and seasoned pros alike.

- **Exploration of Hacking Tools and Vulnerability Detection**  
  Attendees had the opportunity to dive into some of the most commonly used tools in cybersecurity, learning how vulnerabilities are detected and exploited.

- **Live Hacking Demonstration**  
  In one of the most exciting segments of the event, Jasper showcased real-world hacking techniques from an attacker’s perspective. This live demo offered an eye-opening view into the mind of a hacker.

- **Networking Opportunities**  
  The seminar provided ample opportunities for participants to connect with fellow attendees and cybersecurity experts, fostering a collaborative learning environment.

## **Success and Takeaways**

The seminar was not only informative but also interactive. Attendees walked away with a clearer understanding of cybersecurity, equipped with practical knowledge of both offense (hacking) and defense (protective measures).
